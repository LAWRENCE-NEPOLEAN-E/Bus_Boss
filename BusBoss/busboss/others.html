lost properties


Passengers who have lost their belongings during travel in buses may approach Public Relations Officer, MTC over Phone Numbers 23455866 and 23455801 - Extension 236 or by letter with details of articles lost, bus route number, fleet number, date and time of travel and purchased ticket number. The same will be recorded in the PRO's office. In case of the articles found and returned to the office, the same will be intimated to the passenger concerned. The lost and found articles will be returned to the claimant of the articles on getting proper identification of the articles, from the claimant. Unclaimed articles will be disposed once in a year through public auction


Chartered Trip


Chartered trips for companies:


ordinary buses Rs.45/per Km (upto 100 Kms) subject to operation of 100 Kms per day.

For Night trips ie from 11.00 Pm to 4.00 Am 25% extra of above rate shall be charged.


Chartered Trips for private schools /parties:


Twice the LSS bus fare for the actual journey performed by the party subject to a minimum of 100 passengers.

Reservation charges


Rs.200/- per bus / Trip.

If the party detains the bus before the schduled time of departure by more than 15 minutes, Detention Charges at the rate of Rs.150/- for every block of 15 minutes will be collected on the spot for the period exceeding 15 minutes.
